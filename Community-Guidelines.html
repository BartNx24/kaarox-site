Community Guidelines

Last updated: 2026-06-08

These Community Guidelines explain the rules for using Kaarox. They are designed to help keep Kaarox safe, respectful, useful, and trustworthy for users, businesses, professionals, creators, groups, and communities.

By using Kaarox, you agree to follow these Community Guidelines, our Terms of Service, our Privacy Policy, and any other policies that apply to your account or use of the app.

1. Be Respectful

Kaarox is a professional and community-based platform. Users must treat others with respect.

You must not:

  • harass, bully, threaten, or intimidate others;
  • use hate speech or discriminatory language;
  • target people based on race, nationality, religion, gender, disability, age, sexual orientation, or other protected characteristics;
  • shame, humiliate, or abuse other users;
  • repeatedly contact someone after they have blocked you or asked you to stop.

Professional disagreement is allowed, but abuse, harassment, and threats are not allowed.

2. Use Real and Honest Information

Kaarox depends on trust. Users must not mislead others about who they are, what they offer, or what they represent.

You must not:

  • impersonate another person, business, brand, or organisation;
  • create fake accounts to deceive people;
  • use false business details, fake qualifications, fake addresses, or misleading contact information;
  • falsely claim to represent a company, government body, charity, or professional organisation;
  • create fake reviews, fake ratings, fake likes, fake comments, or fake engagement.

Business profiles, personal pages, digital cards, services, posts, groups, events, bookings, and listings should be accurate and honest.

3. No Scams, Fraud, or Deceptive Activity

Kaarox must not be used to trick, exploit, or financially harm others.

You must not:

  • run scams, fake offers, or fraudulent promotions;
  • request money using false information;
  • promote fake investments, fake jobs, pyramid schemes, or get-rich-quick schemes;
  • misuse payment, booking, subscription, or messaging features;
  • send phishing links or try to steal passwords, payment details, verification codes, or personal information;
  • pretend to be Kaarox support or another official representative.

Kaarox may remove content, restrict accounts, or report serious fraud to relevant authorities where appropriate.

4. No Spam or Platform Manipulation

Kaarox is designed for meaningful discovery and connection, not spam.

You must not:

  • send repeated unwanted messages;
  • mass-contact users with irrelevant promotions;
  • create many accounts to bypass limits;
  • artificially increase views, likes, ratings, comments, subscribers, shares, or visibility;
  • misuse automatic sharing, prolonged sharing, discovery, visibility, or promotional features;
  • post the same content repeatedly in a disruptive way;
  • use bots, scripts, scraping tools, or automated abuse.

Business promotion is allowed, but it must be honest, relevant, and respectful.

5. Safe Messaging and Communication

Kaarox messaging should be used for genuine communication.

You must not use messages to:

  • harass, threaten, or abuse users;
  • send sexual, violent, hateful, or exploitative content;
  • send scams, spam, or phishing attempts;
  • pressure users into payments, meetings, or actions they do not want;
  • share private information about someone without permission;
  • bypass another user’s privacy settings, block settings, or communication preferences.

Users can block or report others. Kaarox may restrict messaging access if it is misused.

6. Prohibited Content

You must not post, upload, share, promote, or send content that includes:

  • hate speech or extremist content;
  • threats, violence, or encouragement of harm;
  • graphic violence or gore;
  • sexual exploitation or abuse;
  • nudity or sexually explicit content;
  • child sexual abuse material or any content involving exploitation of minors;
  • illegal goods or illegal services;
  • dangerous weapons, explosives, or instructions to cause harm;
  • drugs or controlled substances;
  • stolen goods or counterfeit products;
  • malware, spyware, phishing tools, hacking services, or harmful technical activity;
  • private personal information shared without consent;
  • content that infringes intellectual property rights.

Kaarox may remove prohibited content and take action against the account.

7. Business, Services, and Professional Conduct

If you use Kaarox to promote a business, service, personal page, booking, job, group, event, or offer, you are responsible for making sure your information is lawful, accurate, and safe.

You must not:

  • advertise services you are not qualified or legally allowed to provide;
  • make false claims about results, prices, availability, certifications, guarantees, or experience;
  • mislead users about refunds, cancellations, delivery, appointments, or business terms;
  • use stolen logos, images, videos, names, or brand materials;
  • promote regulated services without following applicable laws;
  • use Kaarox to avoid consumer protection, tax, employment, safety, or business rules.

Kaarox may ask for additional information or verification where needed.

8. Reviews, Ratings, Likes, and Comments

Reviews, ratings, likes, and comments should reflect real experiences and genuine opinions.

You must not:

  • post fake reviews or fake ratings;
  • review your own business using another account;
  • pay for fake positive reviews or fake engagement;
  • attack competitors with false negative reviews;
  • pressure users to leave dishonest reviews;
  • manipulate likes, comments, views, saves, shares, or subscribers.

Kaarox may remove suspicious or abusive engagement.

9. Privacy and Personal Information

Respect other people’s privacy.

You must not:

  • share someone’s phone number, email, address, private messages, photos, documents, or identity information without permission;
  • record, screenshot, or distribute private conversations in a harmful way;
  • use information found on Kaarox to harass, stalk, spam, or target users outside the app;
  • collect user data without permission;
  • scrape or export user profiles, business cards, messages, posts, contact details, or personal pages.

Users should only share information they are comfortable making visible to others.

10. Location and Discovery Features

Kaarox may use location-based discovery, nearby sharing, city-based visibility, or similar features. These features must be used responsibly.

You must not:

  • use location features to stalk, track, or harass others;
  • fake your location to deceive users;
  • misuse nearby sharing to spam people;
  • attempt to identify someone’s exact private location without consent;
  • share someone else’s location information without permission.

Location-based features are for discovery and networking, not surveillance.

11. Groups, Hubs, Events, and Communities

When using groups, hubs, events, or community features, users must keep discussions safe, respectful, and relevant.

You must not:

  • disrupt groups with spam, abuse, or irrelevant content;
  • post repeated promotional content in a disruptive way;
  • organise harmful, illegal, deceptive, or unsafe activities;
  • harass group members, administrators, or event organisers;
  • create events with false or misleading details;
  • promote unsafe gatherings or misleading offers.

Group administrators may moderate their groups, but Kaarox may also take action if these Guidelines are violated.

12. Jobs, Contractors, and Opportunities

If Kaarox allows job posts, contractor offers, hiring requests, or business opportunities, they must be honest and lawful.

You must not:

  • post fake jobs or fake hiring offers;
  • request unpaid work dishonestly;
  • misrepresent pay, location, role, employer, or working conditions;
  • ask users to pay fees for fake opportunities;
  • discriminate unlawfully;
  • collect personal documents without a legitimate reason.

Users should be careful before sharing identity documents, bank details, or sensitive information.

13. Intellectual Property

Only upload or share content you own or have permission to use.

You must not:

  • use copyrighted images, videos, music, logos, or text without permission;
  • copy another user’s profile, card, post, personal page, or business content;
  • use another company’s brand in a misleading way;
  • sell or promote counterfeit goods.

Kaarox may remove content that appears to infringe intellectual property rights.

14. Safety of Minors

Kaarox must not be used to exploit, endanger, or target minors.

You must not:

  • sexually exploit, groom, or abuse minors;
  • request private images or personal information from minors;
  • bully, threaten, or manipulate minors;
  • promote unsafe activities involving minors;
  • create content that puts children at risk.

Kaarox may immediately remove content and report serious violations to the appropriate authorities where required or appropriate.

15. Account Rules and Misuse

You are responsible for your account.

You must not:

  • share your account with someone who is banned;
  • sell, rent, or transfer accounts without permission;
  • use another person’s account without permission;
  • create new accounts to avoid restrictions;
  • interfere with Kaarox systems, security, or app functionality;
  • attempt to hack, reverse engineer, overload, damage, or disrupt the platform.

Kaarox may suspend or remove accounts involved in misuse.

16. Reporting and Blocking

Users can report content, profiles, posts, messages, groups, events, personal pages, digital cards, or behaviour that may break these Guidelines. Users may also block other users where the app provides this option.

Reports and blocks may be handled under our Report and Block Policy.

False or abusive reports may also lead to account action.

17. Enforcement

Kaarox may take action when these Guidelines are broken. The action depends on the seriousness of the violation, the user’s history, and the risk to others.

Possible actions include:

  • warning the user;
  • removing content;
  • reducing content visibility;
  • disabling certain features;
  • limiting messaging, posting, sharing, booking, or discovery;
  • suspending the account;
  • permanently banning the account;
  • reporting serious cases to relevant authorities where required or appropriate.

Kaarox may act without notice where there is serious risk, illegal activity, fraud, abuse, or harm.

18. Appeals

If your content, account, group, card, page, post, or feature is restricted and you believe this was a mistake, you may contact us at info@kaarox.com.

Please include your account email, the content or action you are appealing, why you believe the decision was wrong, and any helpful evidence.

19. Changes to These Guidelines

Kaarox may update these Community Guidelines from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page. If changes are important, Kaarox may notify users through the app, website, or email.

20. Contact

For safety, moderation, report, appeal, or Community Guidelines questions, contact: info@kaarox.com
