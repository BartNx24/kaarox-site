Refund Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-08

This Refund Policy explains how refunds work for Kaarox subscriptions, trials, and paid digital services.

1. Digital Subscription Service

Kaarox provides digital app services, including business profile features, digital cards, discovery tools, visibility features, messaging, and other subscription-based services. Because these are digital services, payments are generally non-refundable once access to the service has started, except where required by law or where this policy says otherwise.

2. Subscriptions and Renewals

Kaarox may offer monthly and annual subscription plans. Subscriptions may renew automatically unless cancelled before the next renewal date. Cancelling a subscription stops future renewal charges but does not automatically refund fees already paid for the current billing period.

3. Free Trial

Kaarox may offer a free trial. If a trial is offered, the trial period, features included, and whether payment is required after the trial will be shown before the user starts the trial. If the trial converts into a paid subscription, the user is responsible for cancelling before the trial ends if they do not want to be charged.

4. When Refunds May Be Approved

We may approve a refund where:

  • you were charged twice for the same subscription period;
  • you were charged the wrong amount;
  • you were charged after properly cancelling your subscription;
  • your payment was unauthorized;
  • paid features were not activated and we cannot fix the issue;
  • a refund is required by applicable law.

5. When Refunds Are Usually Not Provided

Refunds are usually not provided where:

  • you changed your mind after purchasing;
  • you forgot to cancel before renewal;
  • you did not use the subscription during the paid period;
  • your account was restricted or removed for violating our policies;
  • you selected the wrong plan but received access to the selected plan.

6. Apple App Store and Google Play Purchases

If you purchased a Kaarox subscription through the Apple App Store or Google Play, refund requests must be made through Apple or Google directly. Their refund rules and decision process will apply.

7. PayPal or Card Payments

If you paid directly through Kaarox using PayPal or card payment, you can request a refund by contacting us at the email address below. We may ask for your account email, transaction ID, payment date, plan name, and a short explanation of the issue.

8. Refund Review Time

We aim to review refund requests within 5 to 10 business days. If approved, the refund will usually be returned to the original payment method. The time it takes to appear in your account depends on your bank, card provider, PayPal, Apple, Google, or other payment provider.

9. Effect of Refund

If a refund is issued, Kaarox may cancel or reduce access to the paid subscription features connected to that payment.

10. Contact

For refund requests, contact: info@kaarox.com
