Report and Block Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-08

This Report and Block Policy explains how users can report content, block other users, and how Kaarox may review and moderate accounts, posts, cards, messages, groups, and other content.

1. Purpose of This Policy

Kaarox is designed to help users discover businesses, services, professionals, groups, posts, personal pages, and digital cards. To keep the app safe and useful, users may report harmful content or block other users.

2. What You Can Report

You may report content or behaviour that appears to break Kaarox policies, applicable law, or user safety rules. This may include:

  • fake, misleading, or impersonating profiles;
  • fraud, scams, or suspicious business activity;
  • harassment, threats, bullying, or abusive messages;
  • hate speech or discriminatory content;
  • sexual, violent, exploitative, or harmful content;
  • illegal products, illegal services, or unsafe activity;
  • spam, mass messaging, or unwanted promotions;
  • misleading business cards, posts, groups, or personal pages;
  • copyright, trademark, or intellectual property concerns;
  • content that puts another person’s privacy or safety at risk.

3. How to Report

Where available, you can use the report option inside the app. You may be asked to select a reason and provide extra details. You can also contact us at info@kaarox.com.

When contacting us by email, please include your account email, the content or user being reported, the reason for the report, and any helpful details.

4. What Happens After a Report

After a report is submitted, Kaarox may review the reported content, account, card, post, group, message, or activity. We may use the report details, account information, content history, technical information, and moderation records to understand the issue.

Submitting a report does not guarantee that content will be removed or that an account will be restricted. Kaarox will decide what action is appropriate based on the available information, app policies, and applicable law.

5. Possible Actions Kaarox May Take

Depending on the situation, Kaarox may take one or more of the following actions:

  • remove or hide content;
  • limit the visibility of a card, post, profile, group, or page;
  • disable automatic sharing or discovery features;
  • send a warning to the user;
  • temporarily suspend an account, group, post, card, or feature;
  • permanently ban an account for serious or repeated violations;
  • prevent a user from messaging, posting, sharing, or joining groups;
  • preserve records where needed for safety, legal, or dispute reasons;
  • contact law enforcement or authorities where required or appropriate.

6. Blocking Users

You may block another user where the app provides a block option. Blocking is designed to reduce unwanted contact and visibility between users.

When you block a user, Kaarox may stop or limit some interactions between you and that user. For example, you may no longer see their posts or content, and they may no longer see certain content from you where supported by the app.

7. Limits of Blocking

Blocking may not remove all previous interactions. For example, previous messages, reports, saved content, group activity, shared posts, screenshots, downloaded media, or content already received by other users may still exist.

Blocking also does not prevent Kaarox from keeping records needed for safety, moderation, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or dispute handling.

8. False or Abusive Reports

Reports should be made honestly and in good faith. Misusing the report system, repeatedly making false reports, or reporting users to harass or silence them may result in action against the reporting account.

9. Emergency Situations

If you believe someone is in immediate danger, contact local emergency services or law enforcement. Kaarox reports are not a substitute for emergency services.

10. Appeals

If your account, post, card, group, page, or feature is restricted and you believe this was a mistake, you may contact us at info@kaarox.com. Please include your account email and a clear explanation of why you believe the decision should be reviewed.

11. Privacy of Reports

We generally do not share the identity of the person who submitted a report with the reported user, except where required by law, needed for legal proceedings, or necessary to protect rights and safety.

12. Changes to This Policy

Kaarox may update this Report and Block Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted on this page.

13. Contact

For report, block, moderation, or appeal questions, contact: info@kaarox.com
